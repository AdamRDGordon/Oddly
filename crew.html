⚡ Getting started

From sign-up to first pay cheque in five steps.

The whole thing takes about 10 minutes to set up. Then you're in the Crew.

📱
1. Download and sign up
Create your Oddly Crew profile — your name, a photo, and what jobs you're up for. Takes 5 minutes. Your avatar starts at Rookie Crew. 🌱
👨‍👩‍👧
2. Get your parent on board
Invite your parent to link their account. They set your radius and hour limits. Once they're in, you're cleared for jobs. This is what makes Oddly work — they've got your back.
🔍
3. Browse jobs near you
See real jobs posted by verified neighbours in your area. Lawn mowing, dog walking, car washing — filtered to what you can actually get to. Your parent approves the first job with each neighbour.
🤝
4. Agree on a fair price
The app suggests a fair range based on the job size and local data. You can negotiate — and Oddly helps you do it confidently. Price is locked before you start. Payment is already secured.
🏆
5. Do the job, get paid, level up
Check in when you arrive. Do a great job. Submit your completion photo. Get paid instantly when confirmed. Earn XP, build your CV, and watch your avatar grow.
