How it works

Simple for the Crew, parents and neighbours.

Oddly turns small local tasks into a safe first work experience with clear approval, guided pricing and useful feedback.

🚀

For the Oddly Crew

  1. 1Join with a linked parent account.
  2. 2Browse jobs inside your approved area.
  3. 3Check in, do the job, earn money and XP.
🛡️

For parents

  1. 1Set job types, distance and hour limits.
  2. 2Approve employers before job one.
  3. 3See check-ins, locations and completion photos.
🏡

For neighbours

  1. 1Post the task and receive a price guide.
  2. 2Match with local, approved Crew.
  3. 3Confirm completion and leave kind feedback.
