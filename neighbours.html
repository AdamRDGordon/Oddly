⚡ How it works

Post a job in minutes.

It's straightforward from start to finish. And you're doing something genuinely good for a young person in your neighbourhood.

📝
1. Post your job
Describe what needs doing — lawn size, car type, whatever's relevant. The app uses Google Maps satellite view to help estimate the size and time automatically.
💰
2. Get a fair price suggestion
Oddly suggests a fair range based on job type, size, and local data. Prices reflect teen-appropriate rates — below a professional adult, but fair for the effort involved. The young person can negotiate once.
🔒
3. Pay securely upfront
Funds are held securely by Oddly — "Payment Secured" — and only released when the job is complete and confirmed. No awkward cash conversations. No chasing anyone.
📸
4. Receive photo proof
For visual jobs like lawn mowing and car washing, the Crew member submits a before and after photo. You can see the work is done before confirming completion.
⭐
5. Leave encouraging feedback
No star ratings — just a simple descriptor and an optional note. Your feedback helps a young person build their CV and reputation. Make it count.
🤝 What to expect

Good effort. Not perfection.

When you post a job on Oddly, you're supporting a young person taking their first real step into the working world. Here's how to get the most out of it — for both of you.

✅
Scope is set in writing
The job description, size, and price are all agreed and locked before the job starts. The Crew member does what was agreed — no more, no less. No surprises on either side.
💪
Honest effort, not professional finish
The lawn gets mowed — not landscaped. The car gets washed — not detailed. You're getting the work done by someone learning. The price reflects that. The effort is real.
🌱
You're investing in someone's future
Every job you post is logged on that young person's verified CV. Your feedback shapes their reputation. You're not just getting your lawn mowed — you're helping someone build their career.
🛡️ Trust and verification

You're verified too.

Oddly works because trust runs both ways. Neighbours verify their identity and address before posting. Parents approve you before their child works for you. Here's how it builds over time.

🪪
Identity verified
Your identity and address are verified before you can post a job. Parents see this information before approving their child to work for you.
🏘️
Community endorsements
Verified neighbours can endorse you — "I've known Anna for 10 years and would recommend her to local families." These endorsements are visible to parents considering your job post.
⭐
Trusted Neighbour status
After multiple successful jobs with Oddly Crew members and positive feedback from parents, you earn Trusted Neighbour status. Faster bookings, fewer approvals, stronger community standing.
