Policy

Age-appropriate jobs only.

Oddly is for practical local tasks that young people can do safely. Parents can disable categories at any time.

  • ✅
    Allowed examplesLawn mowing, car washing, pet walks, garden tidy and grocery carrying.
  • 🚫
    Not suitableHazardous tools, overnight work, unsafe locations or tasks outside parent limits.
